Clothing E-commerce Website

Client: Sutramglobal
Role: UX Resarch & Design

Sutramglobal is a startup specializing in selling men’s and women’s clothing, along with a range of perfumes, through its e-commerce platform. The brand focuses on offering trendy and high-quality products to cater to diverse customer preferences.

*This project was undertaken while working at DeviceDisk, for our client Sutramglobal to design their website.

Introduction

Working on the Sutramglobal website was an exciting challenge for me because it was my first time designing an e-commerce platform. The client’s main objective was to create a website that had the look and feel of top clothing brands like Zara, Manish Malhotra, and Fabindia. They wanted a platform where they could sell not just clothes, especially upper body wear, but also accessories like perfumes.

While working on this project, I thoroughly enjoyed collaborating with the client and maintaining the essence of top brands while giving the website its own unique feel. Since the logo was in black and white, I kept the color scheme simple by using only black and white, with subtle touches of grey to maintain a clean and elegant look.

User Flow

Homepage

During our discussions, the client specifically wanted the header design to resemble Zara’s, so I designed it with a similar style while making slight tweaks to fit Sutramglobal’s branding.

For the hero section, I created three sliders showcasing men’s, women’s, and perfume products to give users an immediate sense of the brand’s offerings. Since Sutramglobal is a startup, the client also wanted a website that was simple and easy to navigate. To achieve this, I kept the sections minimal yet effective. I added a ‘Best Products’ section where users could easily browse top-selling items, categorized into men’s, women’s, and perfume collections.

To enhance user engagement, I also included a banner section for promotions, allowing the brand to highlight special offers or festival discounts. Lastly, I introduced a ‘Trending Products’ section, as people tend to follow trends, which would help increase interaction and drive more sales.

Product Detail Page

On the product detail page, I designed two key action buttons: ‘Add to Cart’ and ‘Buy Now.’ This gives users the flexibility to choose how they want to shop. If they want to purchase only that specific product, they can click ‘Buy Now,’ which takes them directly to the checkout page. If they prefer to buy multiple items, they can use ‘Add to Cart,’ allowing them to continue shopping and later proceed to checkout from the ‘My Cart’ page.

To build trust and provide more insight into the products, I included a ratings and reviews section where users can see feedback from other buyers. Additionally, I added a ‘Similar Products’ section to enhance user interaction by suggesting related items they might be interested in, improving both engagement and the overall shopping experience.

My Cart

In the ‘My Cart’ section, users can see all the products they’ve added while shopping. If they want to purchase multiple quantities of the same product, they can easily do so using the quantity selector without having to go back to the product detail page. This makes the shopping process much smoother and more convenient.

Additionally, as users adjust the quantity, the total price updates dynamically, giving them a clear view of their final cost in real time. This small but effective feature enhances the overall user experience by making the checkout process faster and hassle-free

*All products are added here when clicked on "Add to cart" button

Checkout Page

While designing the checkout page, I wanted to improve the user experience by making address management more seamless. So, I introduced a flexible approach for both new and returning users.

For a new user, they simply enter their shipping details at checkout. If they want to save the address for future use, they can check the ‘Add this address to saved addresses’ option. When they do this, the address gets stored as the default address, making future checkouts easier. They can later edit or remove this default address from their saved addresses list if needed.

For a returning user, the checkout form automatically fills in their default address, so they don’t have to enter it manually every time. If they need to use a different address, they can click the ‘Choose from saved addresses’ button. This opens a side panel from the left, displaying all their previously saved addresses. They can quickly select one without leaving the checkout page, keeping the process smooth and distraction-free.

This flow not only enhances convenience but also helps improve business conversions by making the checkout process faster and reducing friction. In just one month after implementing this design, we successfully processed five orders through this improved checkout experience.

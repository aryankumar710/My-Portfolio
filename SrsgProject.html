IT, Media & Broadcasting Website Redesign

Client: SRSG
Role: UX Resarch & Design

A leading IT and Media solutions provider in India, specializing in technology integration and managed services. Initially focused on introducing Apple technology to Indian companies, the company now offers a wide range of solutions across IT, Mobility, Media, Broadcasting, and Entertainment sectors.

*This project was undertaken while working at DeviceDisk, for our client SRSG to redesign their website.

Introduction

When I worked on the SRSG website redesign, my main goal was to enhance the user experience by making important information easily accessible. The previous version wasn’t very clear, which made it difficult for new users to understand what SRSG offered and the overall purpose of the website. To address this, I focused on restructuring the content and improving the information flow to make it more intuitive and user-friendly.

By organizing the content in a way that aligned with user expectations, I ensured that visitors could quickly find the information they needed. This not only helps improve user engagement but also gave users a better understanding of SRSG’s services, ultimately contributing to a more seamless and effective browsing experience. This way, visitors could quickly find what they need, which helps boost engagement and understanding of SRSG’s services.

Hero Section

In the earlier version of the SRSG website, the banner section wasn’t doing a good job of communicating the company’s core solutions. It used unrelated images and lacked a clear description of their objectives, which led to user confusion. To solve this, I redesigned the banner with auto sliders that showcased SRSG’s 5 main solutions. Each slider featured a relevant heading, a brief description, an appropriate image, and a ‘Know More’ button that guided users to the respective solution page.

At the same time, the client wanted to keep the header minimal by displaying only essential information. So, I streamlined the design by retaining key navigation elements in the header and moving secondary details to the footer. This approach kept the header clean and focused while making sure that additional content remained easily accessible.

*New Design

Service Section

While working on the SRSG website, I noticed that their services, which are a critical part of their business, were not highlighted on the homepage. This lack of visibility made it difficult for users to understand the full range of offerings, resulting in lower engagement and missed potential business opportunities. Since showcasing their services was essential for driving user interest and conversions, I added a dedicated section on the homepage that clearly presented their key services.

This new section was designed to draw attention to SRSG’s expertise and solutions, making it easier for users to explore relevant services and navigate to the desired pages. By improving visibility and aligning the homepage with SRSG’s business goals, this change helped create a more informative and user-friendly experience.

*New Design

Achievement Section

*Old Design

While reviewing the SRSG website, I noticed that the achievements section was quite underwhelming and didn’t effectively showcase the company’s success or build trust with users. It lacked visual appeal and didn’t present key metrics in a way that captured attention. Since highlighting achievements is crucial for establishing credibility, I decided to revamp the section by creating a more engaging layout that emphasized SRSG’s milestones and success stories.

I introduced visually appealing elements, organized the information more clearly, and included relevant metrics to make the content more impactful. This new design not only made it easier for users to grasp SRSG’s accomplishments but also helped establish a sense of trust and reliability, ultimately contributing to better user engagement and supporting business growth.
